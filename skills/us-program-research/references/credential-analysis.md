## In-State Tuition Logic

### Eligibility Rules:

| Status                                    | In-State Eligible? | Notes                                     |
|:------------------------------------------|:-----------------:|:------------------------------------------|
| Green Card + 12 months domicile in state  | ‚úÖ Yes            | Must be BEFORE enrollment date            |
| F1 visa (any duration)                    | ‚ùå Generally No   | Even after years of residence             |
| H1-B visa                                 | Varies by state   | Check each state law                      |
| US Citizen                                | ‚úÖ Yes            | Must establish domicile                   |

**Impact:** Typically 40‚Äì60% discount on tuition at public universities.
**Private universities:** Flat rate regardless of residency ‚Äî no benefit from Green Card.

### Calculation to include in document:

For each public university:
1. Find in-state cost per credit hour (official website)
2. Find out-of-state cost per credit hour
3. Calculate total both ways (in-state total, out-of-state total)
4. If candidate has Green Card + moving to state 12+ months before enrollment ‚Üí use in-state
5. Show both in the cost comparison table with a note on eligibility
